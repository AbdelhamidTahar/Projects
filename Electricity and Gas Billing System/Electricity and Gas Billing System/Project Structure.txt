Project Structure

To simplify the implementation of the electricity and gas billing system, the project was divided into separate logical components.

The system is structured as follows:

1. Electricity Calculation Module
- Handles electricity consumption calculation
- Applies tier-based electricity pricing
- Calculates electricity costs independently

2. Gas Calculation Module
- Handles gas consumption calculation
- Applies tier-based gas pricing
- Calculates gas costs independently

3. Taxes and Fees Module
- Handles all taxes and additional charges
- Includes VAT, fixed charges, and other applicable fees

4. Final Billing Module
- Combines electricity and gas results
- Adds taxes and additional fees
- Produces the final invoice

------------------------------------------------------------

Important Design Principle:

Electricity calculations are completely independent from gas calculations.
Gas calculations are also independent from electricity and taxes.

However, in the final stage, all components are merged to produce a single unified invoice.

This design reflects the real billing system used by Sonelgaz in Algeria, where electricity and gas are not treated as separate bills, but rather combined into one unified statement.

------------------------------------------------------------

Conclusion:

The system is divided into:
- Electricity bill module
- Gas bill module
- Taxes module
- Final combined bill

This modular structure ensures clarity, scalability, and easier maintenance while accurately simulating a real-world billing system.
