Billing Rules – Electricity and Gas Calculation System

This system is an educational project inspired by the Algerian company Sonelgaz. It simulates electricity and gas billing based on consumption measured between an old meter index and a new meter index, using a multi-tier tariff structure.

------------------------------------------------------------
⚡ ELECTRICITY BILL CALCULATION
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Consumption Calculation:
- Old meter index is read
- New meter index is read
- Consumption = New Index - Old Index
- The result is multiplied by a coefficient (if applicable) to obtain the billable consumption.

------------------------------------------------------------

2. Consumption Distribution into Tiers:
The total consumption is divided into four tiers:

- Tier 1: up to 125 kWh
- Tier 2: up to 125 kWh
- Tier 3: up to 750 kWh
- Tier 4: remaining consumption after the first three tiers

------------------------------------------------------------

3. Electricity Cost Calculation:
- Each tier has a specific unit price (tariff)
- Cost of each tier = consumption in tier × unit price

Then:
- Tier 1 + Tier 2 costs are grouped together
- Tier 3 + Tier 4 costs are grouped together
- Final electricity cost = sum of both groups

------------------------------------------------------------
🔥 GAS BILL CALCULATION
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Consumption Calculation:
- Old meter index is read
- New meter index is read
- Consumption = New Index - Old Index

------------------------------------------------------------

2. Consumption Distribution into Tiers:

- Tier 1: up to 1125.00 TH
- Tier 2: up to 1375.00 TH
- Tier 3: up to 5000 TH
- Tier 4: remaining consumption after the first three tiers

------------------------------------------------------------

3. Gas Cost Calculation:
- Each tier has a specific unit price
- Cost of each tier = consumption in tier × unit price

Then:
- Tier 1 + Tier 2 costs are grouped together
- Tier 3 + Tier 4 costs are grouped together
- Final gas cost = sum of both groups

------------------------------------------------------------
💰 TAXES AND ADDITIONAL FEES
------------------------------------------------------------

The following are read and included in the calculation:
- Fixed Consumption Charges
- Service Tax
- Housing Tax
- Contribution
- REPE
- RGPE

------------------------------------------------------------

1. VAT 9% Calculation:
Applied on:
- Electricity (Tier 1 + Tier 2)
- Gas (Tier 1 + Tier 2)
- Fixed Consumption Charges
- Service Tax

VAT 9% = Total × 0.09

------------------------------------------------------------

2. VAT 19% Calculation:
Applied on:
- Electricity (Tier 3 + Tier 4)
- Gas (Tier 3 + Tier 4)

VAT 19% = Total × 0.19

------------------------------------------------------------

3. Total VAT:
VAT Total = VAT 9% + VAT 19%

------------------------------------------------------------
🧾 FINAL BILL CALCULATION
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Net Payment (Before Final Adjustment):
Includes:
- Electricity total cost
- Gas total cost
- All fixed charges and taxes

Net Payment = Base Total + VAT Total

------------------------------------------------------------

2. Final Amount to Pay:
Final Amount = Net Payment + (Fixed Consumption Charges × 0.01)

------------------------------------------------------------

📌 RESULT:
The final amount represents the total payment due by the customer, including all taxes, tiers, and additional charges.
